Lord of The Nerds
(The First of its name)

A Virtual Reality game about a nerd kid defending his treehouse from waves of bullies using funny weapons like slingshot, water guns, water ballons and other.

The Fun
Starts Here

The game focuses on handling the issue of bulling out a remarkable sense of humor. Every aspect of the game shouts FUN, the weapons, the eneimes, the music and the voice over.

Fully Immersive
VR Experience

The game is making use of all the technology provided by the head mounted gear to provide a fully immersive feeling so you can relive that funny childhood moment in a cartoonish world.
